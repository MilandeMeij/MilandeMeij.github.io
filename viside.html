Growth: Vision and Identity

Â At the start of the year, these terms were very vague to me. I didn't really realize what my vision and identity were. Therefore it was hard to formulate a vision and identity. I did the CANVAS excersises we were handed, but this didn't really make anything more clear. That's why the vision and identity that are in my PDP are far from perfect.

Throughout the year, I think I got a clearer idea on both my vision and identity. Even though I don't have a real identity yet, I can say that I fully agree with what I have written down. I realize how important both vision and identity are. They don't only help you realize who you are as a designer, and what you want to accomplish which way, but they also make this clear to people around you. I hope I will be able to get an even more clear vision on both.
