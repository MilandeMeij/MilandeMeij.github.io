About me

 Well, my name is Milan Zecca de Meij. My first name is well-known, my second name comes from Ecuador, and is a composition of José and Carlos.
My parents gave me these names because they met some nice people whilst they were travelling the world. But that was before I was born.

I am 19 years of age. I was born in Hilversum and at this moment in time I still live there. I enjoy a lot of things in life, hanging out with friends, gaming, playing football and just relaxing on my own while listening to music.
This year I started my study at the TU/e and I think it has been one of the best years in my life so far.
