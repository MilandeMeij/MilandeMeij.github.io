Vision

Â I think that the most important thing in design is making optimal use of the newest technologies and creating beautiful products using those. Innovation, which is something the TU/e is known for. Creating regular items using new techniques. Or using new technogolies within your products.

Products should go on an engagement with its user. There should be a connection between the user and the product. Products should mostly serve people who are having a difficult time, and therefore connecting with the user is even more important.

With my design, I want to help a lot of people. I want to show people what new technologies are capable of, and what true engagement can do. Technology is evolving rapidly, and it can be used even more optimally than it is used right now.
